Hello, I'm Molly

From local, small businesses to global corporations, my objective remains consistent: strategically adapt my client's mission and vision into an executable plan that resonates with their audience in an authentic way. Sometimes this is as seemingly simple as a unique mission statement, a visual brand identity package, a media plan, or a kickass website. Most times, it's a combination of many ingredients to create the solid foundation needed to effectively sell the brand experience.

While I am not always the one creating the visual elements (that's what creative geniuses like my husband are for), I have proven to be a damn effective conductor of strategic execution. I prioritize putting myself in my clients' shoes and understanding their story as well as their consumer's, while at the same time anticipating long-term needs and coordinating the pieces necessary to to bring a brand to life. Because without a sound strategy guiding the way, a brand will get lost—no matter how good it looks.
